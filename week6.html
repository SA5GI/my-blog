What Went Well

Although this week felt like it passed very quickly, it still included some valuable learning experiences. Simon introduced us to how Supabase can be integrated into our reflective blogs, allowing for quicker and easier editing — and while I didn’t fully understand it yet, it started to make more sense conceptually. My interactive web project, while progressing, is still full of bugs and needs serious refinement.

I need to dedicate more focused time to understanding Supabase properly instead of pushing it aside. My type brief is still underdeveloped and must be prioritised this coming week, especially the poster and interactive component. I also need to finish my hi-fi prototype, as that’s what will feed directly into the coding and development phase. Time management and focus — especially when studying from home — are areas I know I can improve on.

In the upcoming sprint, I plan to finalise my hi-fi prototype and prepare it for the development stage.
